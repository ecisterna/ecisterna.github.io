About me

I am a highly motivated and technically skilled final-year Systems Engineering student from Argentina, with a strong passion for cybersecurity, software development, and solving complex technical challenges. Throughout my academic journey, I have independently built and deployed a variety of real-world projects using Python, SQL, Docker, and Git. These include an intelligent web scraper for extracting property data and a real-time online chat application. Most recently, I collaborated with a cybersecurity lab for my internship, where I conducted vulnerability testing using platforms such as OWASP Mutillidae II, Docker containers, Burp Suite, OWASP ZAP, and wfuzz, focusing on the OWASP Top 10 2021. I'm passionate about security, automation, and building tools that provide real value. My background also includes strong fundamentals in software engineering, databases, and networks, as well as hands-on skills in penetration testing and secure development. I am actively seeking opportunities where I can grow as a Junior Cybersecurity Analyst, SOC Analyst, or Entry-Level Pentester and contribute to a team while continuing to learn and develop my skills. Let's connect if you're looking for someone who is coachable, technically sound, and ready to jump into real-world challenges!
